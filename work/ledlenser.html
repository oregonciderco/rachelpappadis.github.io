Led Lenser

In the spring of 2015, I took a college class where we were put into teams and tasked with meeting with a company and creating an ad campaign for them. Above is the campaign book my team created for Led Lenser. For this project I did the book layout, all of the ad executions, and helped come up with the campaign theme.

This project was done in collaborations with: Cameron Wong, Gibson Toombs, Tashia Davis, and Preston White.
